WHO WE ARE

THE AGENCY unites players, organizers, educators, and industry professionals into one structured federation initiative. We operate with institutional discipline to support Cyprus esports through governance standards, event frameworks, and community development.

OUR VISION

Attack

Establish a reliable competitive ecosystem with official leagues, transparent rules, and long-term player pathways.

Defend

Represent Cyprus in regional and international esports circles while enforcing integrity, professionalism, and inclusion.

Heal

Educate families, schools, and institutions on balanced gaming culture, digital skills, and responsible growth.

OUR JOURNEY
